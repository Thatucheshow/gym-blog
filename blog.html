Uche's gym tips

Plan your Reps

This gives you a plan and allow you to be intentional with your workout regime. It is important you have a workout regimen to allow you achieve your gym goals.

Stretch

Stretching is just as important as working out as it gets the blood pumping and warms up the muscles and helps prevent injury.

Take Your Time

It is imperative you take your time as you workout, this mean listening to your body and taking a break when you need one. This also includes knowing when to stop when you feel pain or discomfort from doing a rep.
